1. Overview

Ultimate Soccer FC LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you:

  • Visit our website at ultimatesoccertrivia.com ("Website")
  • Create an account and use the digital trivia platform at play.ultimatesoccertrivia.com ("Digital Platform")
  • Redeem a QR Premium Card included with your Ultimate Soccer Trivia Card Game
  • Contact us via email or other channels

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our Services, you consent to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account management: Creating and maintaining your Account, authenticating your identity, linking your QR code to your profile
  • Service delivery: Providing access to the Digital Platform, tracking game progress and scores
  • Communication: Sending account-related notifications (e.g., password resets, Terms updates). We will not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage data to improve the Digital Platform, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

5.1 What We Use

We may use the following technologies on the Website and Digital Platform:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Digital Platform to function (e.g., session authentication, remembering login state). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the Website and Digital Platform (e.g., Google Analytics or similar). These collect aggregated, anonymized data.

5.2 What We Do NOT Use

  • We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies
  • We do not use third-party tracking pixels for ad networks
  • We do not sell data collected through cookies

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using the Digital Platform.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your Account is active or as needed to provide the Services. Specifically:

  • Account data (email, username): Retained until you request account deletion or we discontinue the Digital Platform
  • Game activity data: Retained as long as your Account is active
  • Analytics data: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form indefinitely
  • Support correspondence: Retained for up to 2 years after resolution

Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data
  • Regular security reviews

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your Account credentials.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

⚠️ This section is critically important. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

8.1 Age Restriction

The Digital Platform requires users to be at least 13 years of age to create an Account. We implement an age-screening mechanism during account registration. Users who indicate they are under 13 are blocked from creating an Account.

8.2 No Collection from Children Under 13

We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under the age of 13. We do not direct our Digital Platform to children under 13, and the Digital Platform is classified as a "general audience" service with an age gate.

8.3 Physical Product — No Data Collection

The physical card game does not collect any data whatsoever. It is suitable for players of all ages. Only the Digital Platform (which requires account creation) is subject to the age requirement.

8.4 If We Discover a Child's Data

If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will:

  1. Immediately delete the Account and all associated personal data
  2. Revoke access to the Digital Platform for that Account
  3. Take reasonable steps to prevent future occurrences

8.5 Parental Notice

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an Account or provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at info@ultimatesoccerfc.com. We will take prompt action to delete the information.

8.6 Users Ages 13–17

Users between 13 and 17 may create an Account with parental or guardian consent. We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities and to help enforce this Privacy Policy.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), provides California residents with specific privacy rights. While Ultimate Soccer FC LLC is a small business that may not meet the CCPA's jurisdictional thresholds (annual revenue exceeding $25 million, data on 100,000+ consumers, or 50%+ revenue from selling data), we voluntarily extend the following rights to all California residents as a matter of good practice:

9.1 Your California Rights

  • Right to Know: You may request information about what personal data we have collected about you, the sources, purposes, and third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

9.2 We Do Not Sell or Share Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not "share" your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined under the CPRA. Therefore, there is no need for a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link, but we include this disclosure for transparency.

9.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@ultimatesoccerfc.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.
